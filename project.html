    A functional Health App that navigates through healthy and fresh fruits post customed on mutiple frames. I learned how to, overall, create a prototype using different grid patterns and icons to illustrate/playout a webpage app. Moreover, I utilized elements that included "vector network" and "opacity of layer" in order to draw and illuminate certain images.

    🎥 Canva Videos & Designs 🎥

    Educational videos and posters that were published within many different platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and Canvas. I implemented digital design tools into these developments, for instance, lines & shapes, animation, text, audio, and transitions. Furthermore, I have covered numerous video compositions that cover topics like health problems, biodiversity, narrative, language skits and community issues.     🎨 Personal Card Designing 🎨

    Just the perfect and cute handdrawn birthday cards as a gift. I took ordinary index cards and personalized it into unrecognizable work. With only a few markets and colorful tape, different fonts and drawings are framed for a special occasion. I concentrate on individual satisfaction and enjoyment, taking note of presentation, color, and character choice. I get to explore traditional art that can also be created without any technology, producing physical work to others in the community.
